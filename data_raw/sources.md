# data sources

- bnr exchange rates (eur/ron, daily)
  - link: https://www.bnr.ro/nbrfxrates.xml
  - content: official daily exchange rates published by the national bank of romania (xml format)
  - fields used: date, eur rate (ron per 1 eur)

- economic policy uncertainty (epu) index (monthly)
  - link: https://www.policyuncertainty.com/all_country_data.html
  - content: economic policy uncertainty indices by country/region (usually monthly; download as excel/csv)
  - fields used: date (year-month), epu index value
